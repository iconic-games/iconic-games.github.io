What we're about

Iconic is the newest AAA games studio in London. Backed by venture capital the founding team is a mix of AAA experience and AI expertise with founders having worked on games including GTA, Star Wars, Just Cause, Sleeping Dogs and many more and developing cutting edge AI tech at DeepMind, Meta AI Research and more. We aim to be at the forefront of AI empowered games, bringing experiences to players that weren't previously possible and enabling a small team to deliver bigger, better games.

Borja Gonzalez Leon

Founding Research Scientist

Borja is Founding Research Scientist at Iconic AI and PhD from the Department of Computing at Imperial College London. His research focuses on advancing towards AGI through reinforcement learning, embodied grounded language and open-ended AI research. Formerly, he spearheaded research at multiple leading AI labs including Sony AI, Meta, Politecnico di Milano and Universidad Politecnica de Madrid; with applications in multiple industries including gaming, autonomous driving, advertising, and satellite imagery.

Moreover, Borja actively promotes AI research and education. He is founder and lead organiser of the Imperial College Autonomous Reasoning and Learning group in London. His contributions to the field of AI haven’t gone unnoticed and he was included in the 35 under 35 List of Future Leaders by Santander–CIDOB .

Favourite Games

Mount & Blade II, The Witcher III, Total War: Warhammer, Skyrim
