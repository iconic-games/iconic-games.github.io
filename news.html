Mikel joins the team

The worlds youngest ever Kaggle Grandmaster

11th January 2024

Mikel has been working part time with us for a while now in between travelling the world! Now in January we welcomed him as he started full-time. Mikel graduated last year from the University of Cambridge where he read computer science and at 17 he became the worlds youngest Kaggle Grandmaster, Kaggle being the leading Machine Learning competition.

Mikel's favourite games are Nier Automata, Hades, Dishonoured and Life is Strange (I didn't have to bribe him to say that!).

Alba joins the team

Iconic’s first founding engineer

8th December 2023

Joining us is December is Alba, she’s just graduated from the University of Cambridge where she read computer science. For her final year project she built a rendering engine and water simulation system for games. She also spent the summer working for another game developer in Portsmouth before deciding to join Iconic full-time.

Iconic Founded

Company incorporated

24 March 2023

We had a vision for Iconic to build a new type of games studio leveraging AI to empower human creativity rather than replace it, forming a small but elite team of AAA games experience and AI expertise to deliver to players bigger, better games and experiences that previously wouldn't be possible.

John and Junaid were out at GDC in San Francisco talking to the great and the good of the games industry and everyone was telling us don't even think about starting a new AAA studio, it's not possible, you'll need at least a hundred million dollars and AI isn't going to change anything. So of course we incorporated Iconic on the last day of the conference with the confidence that we're not going to see a lot of competition in this area and the firm belief that everything is going to change. We're going to be on the forefront of that change, providing better value and more variety for players and better working experience and real ownership for developers.
